CLEAN PANELS EQUAL MORE ENERGY

WHY YOU NEED CLEAN SOLAR PANELS

Regular cleaning boosts the efficiency of the panels, thus decreasing your monthly bill. Saving you money year-round. Manufacturer warranties recommend regular cleanings, through a precise cleaning process as well that will not void the warranty nor damage the panels. Only enhancing the benefits of solar. Cleaning the panels regularly improves ROI (RETURN ON INVESTMENT). When someone purchases panels, they do so knowing they are investments over time. Cleaning them will give an ROI within a handful of cleanings. NOT CLEANING IS NOT SAVING $$. Therefore not an investment anymore. The aesthetic appeal of clean panels, stands out on top of a house becoming very eye-catching. Appealing to the owner who likes to be the clean looking house on the street, as well as the neighbors.
